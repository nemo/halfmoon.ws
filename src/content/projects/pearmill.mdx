Founded a modern performance marketing agency in 2019. Pearmill has managed performance creative and media for high-growth startups like Ramp, Codecademy, and Sonder, as well as enterprises like Autodesk, Orkin, and Stamps.com.

As cofounder, president, and CTO, I set the technical and operational direction for the company, pairing creative production with media buying so that the two disciplines inform each other rather than operate in silos. Pearmill's model treats creative as the primary lever of performance, producing and iterating on ad concepts at the pace that paid channels actually demand.

The work spans the full spectrum of growth: helping venture-backed startups find product-market fit in their acquisition funnels, and giving established enterprises a faster, more experimental approach to their marketing. Across both, the focus stays the same — building the systems, tooling, and teams that let great creative reach the right audiences efficiently.
